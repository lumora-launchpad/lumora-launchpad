## Notes on reach

- Every token and campaign page already renders an OG card, so pasting a Lumora
  link into a post shows a rich preview automatically. Always post the link.
- Before any public push, seed 5 to 10 real tokens and a couple of campaigns so
  the site is not empty when people arrive. The live ticker and King of the hill
  keep it looking active.
- The strongest pitch to a creator is the 60 percent campaign share. Recruit 3
  to 5 creators first, their audiences become the first traders.
- Post every launch, funded campaign, and graduation. Consistency over volume.
